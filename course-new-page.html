Program Benefits

  • Industry-aligned curriculum designed with industry partners
  • Hands-on lab sessions & real-world projects
  • Exposure to emerging technologies and trends
  • Skill-building workshops & professional certifications
  • Dedicated career guidance & placement support

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for reserved categories)
  • Qualified HRIT HNAT Test or equivalent entrance exam
  • Passion for innovation and engineering solutions
  • Personal interview for final selection

Program Learning Outcomes

PO1Apply Comprehensive Subject Knowledge

Demonstrate a deep understanding of core and advanced concepts within their chosen discipline, enabling them to address complex academic and professional challenges with confidence and competence.

PO2Critical Thinking & Problem Solving

Analyze information objectively, interpret data accurately, and apply logical reasoning to develop innovative and sustainable solutions to real-world problems.

PO3Communicate with Clarity and Impact

Express ideas, technical information, and research findings effectively across written, oral, and digital platforms, tailoring communication to suit diverse audiences and professional settings.

PO4Utilize Modern Tools and Technologies

Employ discipline-specific tools, techniques, and digital technologies with precision and ethical responsibility to enhance productivity and quality in academic and workplace environments.

PO5Collaborate and Lead in a Team Environment

Work productively in multidisciplinary and multicultural teams, demonstrating strong interpersonal skills, leadership qualities, and the ability to manage group dynamics to achieve common goals.

PO6Exhibit Ethical and Professional Integrity

Integrate ethical reasoning and a sense of social responsibility into decision-making processes, maintaining integrity and accountability in both personal and professional spheres.

PO7Pursue Lifelong Learning and Innovation

Develop a mindset of continuous learning and adaptability, staying abreast of advancements in their field and contributing to innovation and entrepreneurial initiatives.

PO8Appreciate Global and Cultural Contexts

Understand and appreciate the impact of global trends and cultural diversity in shaping knowledge systems and professional practices, fostering inclusivity and global citizenship.
