Our Vision

The University of North West Himalayas (UNwH), located in the Village Saongaun Bhgwanpur, Dharkot Road, Thano, scenic and intellectually vibrant city of Dehradun, is a premier institution dedicated to academic excellence, interdisciplinary learning, and global leadership. Established with a vision to create a transformative educational ecosystem, UNwH is committed to empowering students through innovation, research, and inclusive education. With its modern campus nestled amidst the peaceful Himalayan foothills, UNwH combines the best of natural inspiration and cutting-edge infrastructure. The university offers a rich spectrum of undergraduate, postgraduate, doctoral, and professional programs across diverse fields—catering to the academic and professional aspirations of students from across India and beyond.

A New-Generation University with a Holistic Vision At UNwH, education goes beyond textbooks and classrooms. Our approach emphasizes critical thinking, ethical leadership, social responsibility, and environmental sustainability. With a team of highly qualified faculty, world-class labs, digital classrooms, and strong industry partnerships, the university creates an enriching space for intellectual and personal growth.

Our Key Features:

  • -Multidisciplinary Programs in Science, Engineering, Computer, Management, , Education, Humanities, and Health Sciences
  • -Research & Innovation Centers focusing on real-world impact and regional development
  • -Student-Centric Policies including scholarships, mentorship, and career support
  • -Eco-Smart Campus aligned with sustainable development goals (SDGs)
