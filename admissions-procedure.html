Choosing the right university is a life-changing decision and at the University of North West Himalayas (UNwH), we are here to help you every step of the way. Located in the heart of the Himalayan foothills in Dehradun, UNWH offers world-class academics, vibrant student life, cutting-edge research opportunities, and global career pathways. Our admissions process is transparent, student-friendly, and merit-based, designed to identify and support aspiring learners from across India and abroad.

Eligibility Criteria

Each program has specific eligibility guidelines. In general:

  • UG Programs: 10+2 or equivalent with minimum required percentage
  • PG Programs: Bachelor degree in relevant field
  • Ph.D. Programs: Master degree with qualifying entrance/interview
  • International Students: Valid passport, academic equivalence, and English proficiency
