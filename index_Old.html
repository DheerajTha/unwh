The University of North West Himalayas

Overview

Nestled amidst the tranquil beauty of the Himalayan foothills, The University of North West Himalayas is a newly established university run by The Shivalik Hills Foundation Trust is committed to nurturing the next generation of leaders, innovators, and changemakers.

Vision
To convert, Uttarakhand into a model state with high level of quality education for its youth population and to focus on arts, science and culture
Mission
To provide opportunities to the youth for Higher Education according to the requirements and demands after they have received Secondary Education.
