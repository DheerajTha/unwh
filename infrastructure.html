Campus Facilities

A World-Class Campus at the Foothills of the Himalayas

At the University of North West Himalayas (UNWH), (Dehradun), we believe that a dynamic and well-equipped campus environment plays a crucial role in shaping the academic and personal journey of every student. Our campus is designed to be modern, inclusive, safe, and conducive to both learning and holistic development. Nestled in the serene natural beauty of Dehradun, the UNWH campus blends eco-friendly architecture with cutting-edge infrastructure, offering an ideal atmosphere for study, research, recreation, and innovation.

🏢

Academic Infrastructure

  • Smart Classrooms with audio-visual teaching aids & digital boards
  • State-of-the-Art Laboratories for Engineering and Computer Science
  • Advanced Research Centers equipped for interdisciplinary innovation
  • Library & Digital Resource Center with thousands of books and e-journals
  • Language & Communication Lab for personality development
🧑‍💻

IT & Digital Infrastructure

  • High-speed Wi-Fi-enabled Campus
  • Learning Management System (LMS) for digital learning and assignments
  • Dedicated Computer Labs with latest software and hardware
  • Online Examination & Assessment Systems
  • Virtual classrooms & hybrid learning support

Hostel & Residential Facilities

Sports & Recreation
